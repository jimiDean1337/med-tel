As a new company, MedTelPlus has had to work very hard on gaining the trust of our clients and customers. We appreciate all of the wonderful people who choose MedTelPlus services and products. Our Founder and CEO, Kevin Szkolnik, knows first-hand, why having access to fast, quality healthcare is so important.

While serving in the US Navy, Kevin learned that hard work, a positive perspective, and the desire to give to those who need, could help him find the answers to many of lifes problems.
As a Disabled Veteran, Kevin has seen his fair share of mediocre medical services, under-trained, under-motivated medical staff, and people in dire need of affordable healthcare (with an emphasis on the 'CARE'); So Kevin took everything he had learned while serving his country and applied it to his business-model when building MedTelPlus.
